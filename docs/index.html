TapPay Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the TapPay mobile application ("TapPay", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using TapPay you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information we collect

To provide payments, identity, and notification features, TapPay collects:

  • Account information — your name, email address, phone number, and a securely-hashed password supplied during sign-up.
  • Wallet & transaction data — your wallet balance, transaction history, transfer amounts, descriptions, and recipient identifiers (UUID or phone number) for transfers you initiate or receive.
  • Device information — a device identifier (Android ID or iOS identifierForVendor), platform (Android/iOS), OS version, and app build, used to bind your account to a device for security.
  • Push notification token — a Firebase Cloud Messaging registration token that identifies your install for delivery of notifications such as "Payment Received".
  • Authentication data — a locally-stored, salted-hash of any in-app passcode you set. Biometric data (Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint) is processed entirely on-device by the platform; TapPay never receives or stores biometric templates.

2. Permissions we request and why

  • Camera (android.permission.CAMERA, NSCameraUsageDescription) — to scan QR codes during payments. Frames are processed in-memory; we do not store photos or video.
  • NFC (android.permission.NFC) — to send and receive payment tokens by tapping phones together (Android only).
  • Contacts (android.permission.READ_CONTACTS, NSContactsUsageDescription) — read locally to help you pick a recipient by phone number. Contacts are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Notifications (android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — to display payment and security alerts.
  • Biometric / Face ID (android.permission.USE_BIOMETRIC, NSFaceIDUsageDescription) — to authorize sign-in and outgoing payments. Biometric verification happens on-device.
  • Internet (android.permission.INTERNET) — to communicate with our payment backend.
  • Photo library (NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription, NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription) — to set a profile picture or save a receipt to your gallery, only when you explicitly choose to.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and authenticate your TapPay account.
  • To process peer-to-peer payments, top-ups, and withdrawals you initiate.
  • To deliver real-time notifications about money received, payments sent, or security events.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • To comply with applicable financial, tax, and AML/KYC regulations.

4. Third parties we share data with

We share only what's necessary for the service to function:

  • Firebase (Google) — Cloud Messaging (push tokens and message routing). Subject to Google's Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Our payment backend — hosted at nfcsender.up.railway.app, which receives the account and transaction data described in §1 in order to settle transfers and maintain wallet balances.
  • Payment processors — when you top up or cash out, we share the transaction amount and account details required by the processor to complete the funding/payout.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to advertisers.

5. Data retention

Account, wallet, and transaction records are retained for as long as your account is active and for any additional period required by law (typically up to 7 years for financial records). Push tokens are deleted when you sign out or uninstall the app. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time using the contact details below.

6. Security

All network traffic between the app and our backend uses HTTPS. Passcodes are stored only as salted SHA-256 hashes on the device. Access tokens are stored in the platform's secure preferences and are short-lived; refresh tokens are rotated on use. Biometric authentication is performed by the operating system; we never receive your biometric data.

7. Children's privacy

TapPay is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data, subject to legal retention.
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. International transfers

Our backend and Firebase services may process data in regions outside your country of residence. Where such transfers occur, we rely on adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses to protect your data.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notice or email where reasonable.
